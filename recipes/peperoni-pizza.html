Peperoni Pizza

"Pepperoni pizza originates from North America. It is a pizza with a base of tomato sauce, mozzarella and pepperoni, a type of cured salami made from pork and beef mixed and seasoned with paprika. In this recipe I tell you how to make a delicious pepperoni pizza. "

If we look in the translator or in an Italian dictionary for the meaning of "pepperoni" it is very likely that we will not find it. On the o ther hand, if we search for pepperoni it will appear as the plural of peperone (which is peppers). The Americans must have called the sausage pepperoni because they put paprika on the meat to stuff it and thus make this peculiar salami. When cooked it has a very intense flavor. It is not a pizza that is eaten in Italy, although there are pizzas with salami of different types that are delicious. We can find pepperoni spicy or not spicy, we can even find it smoked. The choice is up to the consumer.

Ingrdients

  • 100 gr. pepperoni
  • 100 gr. of salami
  • gr. grated mozzarella cheese
  • can of crushed tomato
  • 100 gr. grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 homemade pizza dough base
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Peper

Steps

  1. Begin by preparing the homemade sauce for your pepperoni pizza. Although some opt for the fried tomato, we prefer the crushed tomato. Add olive oil to a pan and when it is hot add the tomato with salt, pepper and oregano to taste, if you wish add some fresh basil leaves for more flavor.
  2. Stir and cook over very low heat until the sauce thickens. If the acidity point of the tomato stands out too much, correct it with a pinch of sugar. When the sauce is almost ready, turn on the oven at 200ÂºC.
  3. Roll out the homemade dough that you have prepared and place it on the tray covered with parchment paper. Prick the dough with a fork so it doesn't rise. Then, cut the pepperoni and salami into thin slices.
  4. Cover the base with the sauce you have prepared and layer with the grated mozzarella cheese, pepperoni and salami slices. Top with Parmesan cheese to taste and place in the oven for 15 minutes or until perfectly golden.
